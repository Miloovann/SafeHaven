Decent housing

According to the government of United Kingdomthe requirements for decent homes are as follows:

1. Containing no hazard that is assessed as serious
2. None of the key building components are old and, because of their condition and need replacing or major repair
3. One or none other building components are old and, because of their condition, need replacing or major repair.
4. It provides a reasonable degree of thermal comfort
5. Have at least four of the following:

a. a reasonably modern kitchen (20 years old or less)
b. a kitchen with adequate space and layout
c. a reasonably modern bathroom (30 years old or less)
d. an appropriately located bathroom and WC
e. adequate insulation against external noise (where external noise is a problem)
f. adequate size and layout of common areas for blocks of flats.
